FREQUENTLY ASKED

Common questions about ordering, samples, and working with Enzo Rossetti.

How can I request samples?

You can request samples through our contact form above, by emailing sales@enzorossetti.com, or by visiting one of our showrooms. We offer complimentary samples for professionals and a nominal fee for private clients. Sample requests are typically dispatched within 2–3 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide through our network of authorized distributors. Delivery times and costs vary by location. For international orders, please contact our sales team for detailed shipping information and to find your nearest authorized dealer.

What is the lead time for orders?

Standard lead times are typically 4–6 weeks for in-stock items and 8–12 weeks for special orders or custom formats. Large commercial projects may require additional time. We recommend contacting us early in your project planning to ensure timely delivery.

Do you offer trade discounts?

Yes—our trade program supports architects, interior designers, and construction professionals with preferential pricing, priority sample service, and dedicated project support. Apply by contacting trade@enzorossetti.com.

Can I visit a showroom without an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend booking an appointment to ensure dedicated time with our consultants. This also allows us to prepare materials specific to your project.

What technical documentation is available?

We provide installation guidelines, maintenance recommendations, CAD files, BIM objects, and full specification sheets. Access these in the Professionals section or request them from technical@enzorossetti.com.
