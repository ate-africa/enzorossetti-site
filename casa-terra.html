Casa Terra

Earth. Texture. Warmth.

Inspired by the raw beauty of natural stone and the organic textures of earth, Casa Terra brings authenticity and warmth to contemporary interiors. This collection celebrates material honesty through carefully crafted ceramic surfaces.

VIEW ALL SERIES

ABOUT CASA TERRA

Casa Terra explores the relationship between natural materials and modern living. Each series in this collection draws from geological formations, weathered surfaces, and the tactile qualities of stone and earth.

The collection features matt and textured finishes that emphasize materiality and depth. Subtle color variations and surface irregularities are intentional, reflecting the authentic character of natural stone while maintaining the technical performance of ceramic.

"Casa Terra celebrates imperfection as beauty, bringing the grounding presence of natural materials into refined modern spaces."

KEY ATTRIBUTES

Matt & Textured Finishes

Authentic surface treatments that emphasize tactility and natural character, from soft matt to deeply structured finishes.

Stone & Concrete Looks

Inspired by limestone, sandstone, travertine, and weathered concrete. Each series captures distinct geological characteristics.

Durable & Practical

High-performance porcelain technology ensures resistance to wear, staining, and moisture while maintaining aesthetic integrity.

Indoor & Selected Outdoor

Suitable for residential and commercial interiors, with selected series certified for covered exterior applications.

SERIES IN THIS COLLECTION

Each series within Casa Terra offers distinct aesthetic and technical characteristics, unified by a commitment to natural materiality.

Pietra Antica

Ancient stone. Timeless character.

Inspired by weathered limestone and travertine, Pietra Antica captures the gentle irregularities and warm tones of aged stone. Available in four natural shades from ivory to taupe.

Matt Finish Structured Surface 30×60, 60×60 cm

Suggested applications: Living spaces, bedrooms, bathrooms, hospitality interiors

Sabbia

Desert sand. Soft textures. Warmth.

Sabbia evokes the fine-grained texture of windswept sand and sedimentary rock. Its soft, tactile surface and warm color palette create inviting, grounded spaces. Available in five earth tones.

Soft Matt Fine Texture 60×60, 60×120 cm

Suggested applications: Residential floors, feature walls, commercial interiors

Cemento Naturale

Raw concrete. Industrial elegance.

Inspired by poured concrete and industrial surfaces, this series balances urban aesthetic with natural imperfections. Subtle color variations and micro-textures create depth and authenticity.

Matt Finish Micro-textured 60×120, 80×80 cm

Suggested applications: Contemporary living spaces, offices, retail, hospitality

Argilla

Fired clay. Mediterranean warmth.

Argilla celebrates the tradition of terracotta and fired clay with contemporary refinement. Rich, warm tones and subtle surface variations evoke Mediterranean heritage while meeting modern performance standards.

Natural Matt Textured 30×60, 60×60 cm

Suggested applications: Residential interiors, restaurants, boutique hospitality

Roccia Grigia

Grey stone. Nordic restraint.

Inspired by Nordic granite and slate, Roccia Grigia offers cool grey tones with subtle mineral veining. Its refined character suits contemporary minimalist interiors and architectural statements.

Matt Finish Light Structure 60×60, 60×120 cm

Suggested applications: Bathrooms, kitchens, contemporary living spaces, offices

INSTALLATION & CARE

Proper installation ensures optimal performance and longevity of Casa Terra tiles. We recommend working with experienced tile installers familiar with large-format porcelain.

1

Substrate Preparation

Ensure substrate is level, clean, and properly primed. Use appropriate adhesive for tile format.

2

Joint Width

Minimum 2-3mm joint width recommended. Use appropriate grout color to complement tile tone.

3

Daily Maintenance

Clean with pH-neutral detergent and warm water. Avoid abrasive cleaners on textured surfaces.
