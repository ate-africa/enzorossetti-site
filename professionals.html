INSTALLATION GUIDELINES

Professional installation recommendations to ensure optimal performance and longevity.

Surface Preparation

Substrate Requirements

Substrates must be clean, dry, level, and structurally sound. Maximum deviation: 3mm over 2m for floors, 2mm over 2m for walls. Remove all contaminants, curing compounds, and existing coatings.

Moisture Testing

Concrete substrates: maximum 3% moisture content (calcium carbide method) or 75% RH (in-situ probe). Test in accordance with ASTM F2170 or equivalent local standards.

Priming

Apply appropriate primer to porous or uneven substrates. Use epoxy-based primers for non-porous surfaces. Allow primer to cure according to manufacturer specifications.

Installation Method

Adhesive Selection

Use C2-class cementitious adhesive for standard applications. For large-format tiles (≥60×60cm), use C2S1 or C2S2 adhesive with deformable properties. Rapid-set adhesives for fast-track projects.

Application Technique

Apply adhesive using notched trowel appropriate to tile size (8-10mm for standard, 10-12mm for large format). Back-buttering required for tiles ≥60×60cm. Maintain minimum 95% adhesive coverage.

Joint Width

Minimum 2mm for rectified tiles, 3-5mm for standard tiles. Perimeter and movement joints required every 8-10m or as per structural engineer specifications. Respect all building expansion joints.

Grouting

Grout Selection

Use CG2 cementitious grout for standard applications. Epoxy grout (RG) recommended for wet areas, commercial kitchens, and high-traffic zones. Match grout color to tile for seamless appearance.

Application

Apply grout after adhesive has fully cured (minimum 24-48 hours). Work diagonally across joints. Remove excess immediately with damp sponge. Avoid over-wetting polished surfaces.

Curing

Allow grout to cure for minimum 24 hours before light traffic, 7 days before full loading. Protect from rapid drying, direct sunlight, and freezing temperatures during curing period.

Special Applications

Underfloor Heating

All Enzo Rossetti tiles suitable for underfloor heating systems. Use flexible adhesive and grout. Commission heating system before installation, allow to cool completely before tiling.

External Facades

Mechanical fixing systems required for vertical external applications above 3m. Consult structural engineer for wind load calculations. Ensure adequate drainage and ventilation cavity.

Wet Rooms & Pools

Apply tanking membrane to substrate before tiling. Use slip-resistant finishes (R11-R13). Ensure proper falls to drainage points (minimum 1:80). Use epoxy grout for pool applications.

MAINTENANCE RECOMMENDATIONS

Professional cleaning and maintenance protocols to preserve tile appearance and performance.

Routine Cleaning

Daily Maintenance Sweep or vacuum to remove loose dirt and debris. Damp mop with pH-neutral cleaner diluted according to manufacturer instructions. Avoid acidic or alkaline cleaners on polished surfaces.

Weekly Deep Clean Use microfiber mop with recommended cleaning solution. Pay attention to grout lines and textured surfaces. Rinse thoroughly with clean water to prevent residue buildup.

Commercial Environments Increase cleaning frequency based on traffic levels. Use automatic scrubber-dryers for large areas. Implement entrance matting systems to reduce dirt ingress.

Surface Protection

Stain Prevention Wipe spills immediately, particularly acidic substances (wine, citrus, coffee). Apply penetrating sealer to grout joints in high-risk areas. Re-seal grout annually in commercial applications.

Scratch Prevention Use furniture pads under table and chair legs. Avoid dragging heavy objects across tile surface. Place protective mats in high-traffic entry points.

UV Protection Enzo Rossetti tiles are UV-stable and colorfast. However, use window treatments to minimize heat stress in areas with intense direct sunlight, particularly on external facades.

Restoration

Deep Stain Removal For stubborn stains, use poultice method with appropriate solvent. Test in inconspicuous area first. Avoid abrasive cleaning pads on polished finishes.

Grout Refresh Regrout joints if cracking or discoloration occurs. Clean grout lines with specialized cleaner before resealing. Consider epoxy grout for long-term performance.

Professional Service For commercial installations, schedule periodic professional deep cleaning and inspection. We can recommend certified maintenance contractors in your region.

Recommended Products

  • pH-neutral ceramic tile cleaner (pH 7-8)
  • Microfiber mops and cloths (non-abrasive)
  • Penetrating grout sealer (water-based)
  • Soft-bristle brush for textured surfaces

Products to Avoid

  • Acidic cleaners (vinegar, lemon juice, bathroom cleaners)
  • Abrasive powders and scouring pads
  • Wax-based or oil-based cleaners
  • Bleach on colored grout or textured surfaces

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common specification and technical questions answered by our product experts.

What is the maximum size tile suitable for floor applications?

All Enzo Rossetti tiles up to 120×280cm are suitable for floor applications when installed correctly with appropriate adhesive. Large-format tiles (≥60×120cm) require flexible C2S1 or C2S2 adhesive and back-buttering. Substrate must be level to within 3mm over 2m. For commercial high-traffic areas, we recommend maximum 120×120cm format for optimal performance.

Are Enzo Rossetti tiles suitable for external use?

Yes, all our porcelain tiles are frost-resistant and suitable for external applications in accordance with EN 14411. Water absorption is ≤0.5%, meeting requirements for outdoor use in all climates. For external floors, specify tiles with R11-R13 slip resistance rating. For vertical facades, mechanical fixing systems are required for heights above 3m. Consult our technical team for specific project requirements.

What is the lead time for project orders?

Standard stock items: 2-4 weeks for quantities up to 500m². Custom orders and special formats: 6-8 weeks. Large commercial projects (≥1000m²): 8-12 weeks including quality control and batch matching. We recommend early engagement during design development phase to ensure material availability aligns with project schedule. Samples available within 5-7 working days.

How do I specify for LEED or BREEAM certification?

Enzo Rossetti tiles contribute to multiple LEED v4 and BREEAM credits including Materials & Resources (recycled content, regional materials), Indoor Environmental Quality (low-emitting materials), and Innovation (EPD documentation). We provide complete environmental documentation including EPDs, HPDs, and Declare labels. Our technical team can assist with credit calculations and documentation submission. All tiles meet California Section 01350 VOC emissions standards.

Can tiles be installed over existing tile?

Installation over existing tile is possible if the existing surface is sound, well-bonded, and level. Surface must be mechanically abraded or primed with epoxy primer to ensure adequate bond. This approach is not recommended for wet areas or where substrate integrity is questionable. Maximum build-up height must be considered for door clearances and transitions. We recommend removal of existing tile for optimal long-term performance, particularly in commercial applications.

What warranty do you provide for commercial projects?

Enzo Rossetti provides a 10-year manufacturer warranty against defects in materials for commercial installations when installed according to our guidelines by certified installers. Warranty covers manufacturing defects including dimensional variation, structural integrity, and color consistency. Excludes damage from improper installation, inadequate maintenance, or misuse. Extended warranties available for large-scale projects. Register projects within 30 days of completion to activate warranty.

Do you provide on-site technical support during installation?

Yes, we offer on-site technical consultation for projects over 500m² or complex installations including facades, large-format tiles, or special applications. Our technical representatives can attend pre-installation meetings, conduct substrate inspections, and provide guidance during critical installation phases. This service is complimentary for major commercial projects. Contact our professional services team to arrange site visits and discuss project-specific requirements.

How should I handle batch variation and shade matching?

All ceramic and porcelain tiles exhibit natural batch variation. Order sufficient quantity from single production batch when possible. Tiles are calibrated and shade-sorted during manufacturing; check batch numbers on packaging. For large projects, we recommend ordering 10% excess for future repairs from the same batch. Mix tiles from multiple boxes during installation to blend any subtle variation. Store reserve tiles in controlled environment for long-term projects. We maintain batch records for 5 years to facilitate future matching.
