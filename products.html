CONCRETE LOOK TILES

Industrial refinement meets contemporary design. Our concrete effect tiles capture the raw, architectural character of poured concrete with precision and subtlety.

From smooth, polished surfaces to textured, weathered finishes, each series offers the aesthetic of industrial concrete without the weight or maintenance concerns.

Perfect for urban lofts, commercial spaces, and modern interiors seeking an honest, material-driven aesthetic. Available in neutral greys and warm cement tones.

WOOD LOOK TILES

The warmth and character of natural timber, reimagined in durable ceramic and porcelain. Our wood effect tiles capture authentic grain patterns and rich tonal variations.

From light oak and ash to deep walnut and weathered reclaimed wood aesthetics, each series offers the beauty of timber without concerns of moisture, scratching, or maintenance.

Ideal for residential and hospitality interiors. Suitable for wet rooms, high-traffic areas, and underfloor heating. Available in plank formats with subtle texture.
