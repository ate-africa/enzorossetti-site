DEALER PROGRAM FAQ

What is the minimum order requirement?

Initial order requirements vary by territory and are discussed during the application process. We work with partners to establish realistic targets based on market potential and business capacity.

Is there an exclusivity requirement?

We offer territorial exclusivity to qualified dealers who meet performance benchmarks. This protects your investment and ensures focused market development within your designated area.

What training is provided?

Comprehensive initial training covers product knowledge, technical specifications, installation guidelines, and sales techniques. Ongoing training includes new collection launches, advanced technical workshops, and annual dealer conferences.

How long does the application process take?

From initial application to partnership launch typically takes 4-8 weeks. This includes application review, discussions, showroom visit or virtual presentation, agreement finalization, and initial training.

What marketing support is included?

Dealers receive professional photography, digital assets, printed materials, showroom display systems, co-branded marketing campaigns, social media support, and access to our media library. We also provide marketing development funds for qualified partners.

Can I visit your showroom before applying?

Yes, we encourage potential partners to visit our flagship showrooms in Milan, London, or Paris. Appointments can be arranged through our dealer relations team to ensure dedicated time with our specialists.
