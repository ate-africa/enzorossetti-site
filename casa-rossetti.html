PREMIUM COLLECTION

CASA ROSSETTI

Signature finishes for premium spaces.

Refined Surfaces, Architectural Impact

Casa Rossetti represents the pinnacle of our ceramic craftsmanship. Inspired by the world's most prestigious marbles and natural stones, this collection brings sophisticated elegance to high-end residential and commercial interiors.

Each surface is meticulously designed to capture the subtle veining, depth, and luminosity of natural stone, while offering the superior performance and consistency of advanced porcelain technology.

From polished statement floors to soft-matt feature walls, Casa Rossetti transforms spaces with quiet confidence and enduring beauty.

KEY ATTRIBUTES

Premium Finishes

Polished, soft-matt, and lappato surfaces designed to enhance light and create visual depth in sophisticated interiors.

Marble Inspired

Authentic recreations of Calacatta, Statuario, and other prestigious marble varieties with faithful veining and color depth.

Large Formats

Available in oversized slabs up to 120×280 cm, minimizing grout lines and maximizing visual continuity.

Feature Surfaces

Ideal for statement floors, feature walls, reception areas, and high-impact architectural elements.

Installation Guidelines

Substrate Preparation

Ensure substrate is clean, level, and structurally sound. Large format tiles require perfectly flat surfaces with maximum deviation of 3mm per 3m.

Adhesive Selection

Use high-quality flexible adhesive suitable for porcelain. Apply using notched trowel with full bed coverage for large format tiles.

Grouting

Minimum 2mm joint width recommended. Use epoxy grout for wet areas and high-traffic commercial applications.

Expansion Joints

Install movement joints every 8-10m and at perimeter edges, doorways, and transitions to other floor finishes.

Care & Maintenance

Daily Cleaning

Sweep or vacuum regularly. Clean with pH-neutral detergent and water. Avoid abrasive cleaners or acidic products.

Polished Finishes

Wipe spills immediately to prevent water marks. Use microfiber cloth for streak-free shine. Periodic professional maintenance recommended.

Matt & Textured Finishes

More forgiving of minor scratches. Regular cleaning sufficient to maintain appearance. Suitable for high-traffic areas.

Stain Removal

Non-porous surface resists most stains. For stubborn marks, use appropriate specialist cleaner. Test in inconspicuous area first.
