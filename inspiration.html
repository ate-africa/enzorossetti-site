DESIGN CONSIDERATIONS

Key factors to consider when selecting tiles for different room types.

Finish Selection

Polished finishes reflect light and enhance spaciousness. Matt finishes provide slip resistance and hide minor wear.

  • • Polished: Living rooms, lobbies
  • • Matt: Bathrooms, kitchens
  • • Structured: Wet areas, outdoors

Format & Size

Large formats create seamless surfaces ideal for open spaces. Smaller formats suit compact rooms and detailed layouts.

  • • 120×120: Living, commercial
  • • 60×120: Versatile, all rooms
  • • 30×60: Compact spaces

Maintenance

Consider cleaning requirements based on room function. High-traffic areas benefit from stain-resistant surfaces.

  • • Easy clean: Commercial, kitchens
  • • Stain resistant: Dining, offices
  • • Low maintenance: All spaces

Heating Systems

All Enzo Rossetti tiles are compatible with underfloor heating. Ceramic conducts heat efficiently.

  • • Excellent conductivity
  • • Energy efficient
  • • Suitable for all rooms
